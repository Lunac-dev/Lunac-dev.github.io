About

Why us?

Glance Network is a Practice-based server that started around July 2019. Currently, there are Practice, SoupPvP, MLGRush, and UHC game modes, and we are also holding events. If you like minigames and PvP then try joining us and see if you like it or not.

Practice

One of our biggest gamemode in the server, Practice. It has a wide selection of HCF Kits to choose from, including Duel and even kits for mini-games such as SkyWars. We also have occasional tournaments with prizes.

MLGRush

MLGRush was created for players who can't play happily due to EU server latency; it has KBs such as SpieleOase and AntiAC, and uses the same maps.

SoupPvP

There are many kits to fight with in SoupPvP. It's a lot of fun to play around with tactics and make the most of kit. There is also a ranking system available at https://soup.glance.top/

UHC

In Ultra-Hardcore, players cannot regenerate health from food and must use potions, suspicious stews or golden apples to regain lost health. While a UHC play through could theoretically last indefinitely, the primary goal is defeat all of the other players. Glance Network has over 70 scenarios to choose from, and to keep you from getting bored.
